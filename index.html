A bit about me

At 19 I moved to London to study Philosophy at University. After two years of my course I decided to take time out in order pursue a career opportunity in fashion, working as a model. For two years I worked full-time travelling to work for clients in Europe. Eventually I found myself at a complete loss. I felt totally unfulfilled and unaffirmed by work involving uninteresting facts about myself.

So I quit, finished my degree, moved back to London, picked up a bunson burner to pay rent and lived under the proviso that I was to find my next calling! After pursuing a few ideas, I was introduced to web development by a friend working in a front-end position. The prospect of working with software excited me tremendously as it offered a plane for so many of my interests to coincide. A space in which creativity, functionality and ideology coalign.
